About Empire Equity Holdings LLC

Empire Equity Holdings LLC was founded by Yveson Laurore, a healthcare performance improvement professional and real estate investor with a strong background in systems, accountability, and operational excellence.

After years of studying multifamily real estate and working through the challenges of acquiring a first asset, Empire Equity Holdings LLC closed on its first multifamily portfolio in New Britain, Connecticut.

That experience reinforced one core belief:

“Real estate success is not created at closing. It is created through disciplined operations after closing.”

Why We Invest

We invest in workforce housing because safe, well-operated housing is essential to strong communities. Our focus is on acquiring and improving multifamily properties where better systems, stronger tenant quality, and disciplined asset management can create long-term value.

Advisory Services

Multifamily Performance Audit

Many small multifamily owners do not have a property problem. They have an operations problem.

The Multifamily Performance Audit helps small multifamily owners identify where NOI is being lost and where operational improvements can create measurable value.

What We Review

  • Rent roll performance
  • Occupancy and vacancy trends
  • Rent collection patterns
  • Delinquency risk
  • Lease expiration exposure
  • Maintenance spend
  • Turnover costs
  • Utility expenses
  • Property management performance
  • NOI improvement opportunities

Who This Is For

  • Small multifamily owners
  • Out-of-state investors
  • New landlords
  • Owners with underperforming assets
  • Investors preparing to stabilize or refinance a property
